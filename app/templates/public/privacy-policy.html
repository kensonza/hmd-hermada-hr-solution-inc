Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to the website of HMD Hermada HR Solutions Corporation. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using our website.

By accessing or using our website, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide through our website forms and services.

1. Newsletter Subscription

When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect:

  • Email Address

This information is used to send updates, announcements, and newsletters related to our services.

2. Contact / “Let’s Talk” Form

When you contact us through our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Message content

In some cases, the message content may include additional information voluntarily provided by the sender, such as a phone number or other contact details.

3. Website Analytics

We use Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

Google Analytics may collect information such as:

  • Approximate geographic location (based on IP address)
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the website
  • Device and browser type
  • General usage data

This information helps us improve our website and user experience.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and understand where our visitors are coming from.

Essential Cookies

These are necessary for the website to function properly, such as maintaining your preferences (e.g., your choice to accept or dismiss the cookie banner).

Analytics Cookies

We use services like Google Analytics to collect anonymous information about how visitors interact with our site. This helps us improve our content and service offerings.

Security

We implement technologies like Google reCAPTCHA v3 to protect our contact forms from spam and abuse, which may also utilize cookies for risk analysis.

Managing Your Preferences

You can choose to accept or decline cookies through the interactive banner on our homepage. Additionally, most web browsers allow you to manage or delete cookies through their settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To respond to inquiries submitted through the contact form
  • To send newsletters and updates to subscribers
  • To improve the functionality and performance of our website
  • To analyze website traffic and visitor behavior
  • To enhance user experience

Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect the personal information submitted to us. These measures include:

  • Secure website infrastructure
  • Controlled access to collected information
  • Standard security practices to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse

However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services such as Google Analytics to analyze website usage.
These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

We encourage users to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information you provided
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Unsubscribe from newsletters at any time

To make such requests, please contact us using the information provided below.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, technologies, or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you may contact us through our website contact form, via email at ask@hmdhermada.com, or through the official communication channels of HMD Hermada HR Solutions Corporation.
