HMD Hermada HR Solutions began with a shared vision to create a people-focused company that drives business growth through talent and development. The company started as a manpower agency, helping businesses find the right people quickly. Over the years, HMD Hermada grew into a full-service HR solutions partner providing Corporate Training, Leadership Development, Team Building, Recruitment, and Headhunting. More recently, creative solutions have been added to address the needs of our clients.

  • Deliver timely and customized HR solutions that develop leaders, build strong teams, and connect organizations with the right talent.
  • Deliver #TheGoldStandard in speed, quality, and impact.
